Erlang mixture approximations

The Erlang basis functions are given by

\[ \ell_m(t) = b_m t^m \exp(-at), \qquad b_m = \frac{a^{m+1}}{m!}, \]

where \(b_m\) is a normalization factor, and the rate parameter \(a\) is a hyperparameter.
